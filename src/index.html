ABOUT US

Emgwen Technical and Vocational College is a leading institution located in Kilibwoni Area in Nandi County. It is fully funded by the National Government Constituency Fund.

Our college is dedicated to providing quality education and skills training to our students, equipping them with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in their chosen careers. We offer a range of courses in various fields, including Engineering, Business, Information Technology, Hospitality, and Agriculture.

At Emgwen Technical and Vocational College, we pride ourselves on our highly qualified and experienced faculty, who are committed to ensuring that our students receive the best possible education. We also provide state-of-the-art facilities and equipment to enhance the learning experience for our students.
